Privacy Policy
This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on the Morse - Vibration Messenger (hereinafter referred to as 'MorseVibes') app.
Information We Collect
When creating an account, you will be asked to enter a username and a phone number. This information is used for logging in, password resets, and facilitating communication between users. We also collect device ID information to facilitate push notifications.
Technical Basics
We store user interactions, including screen taps and user activities on our servers provided by Google Firebase. This data helps us improve our service and offer you a personalized experience.
Push Notifications
We send you push notifications if you choose to receive them for reminding you of certain activities on our app. We send push notifications from time-to-time in order to update you about any events or promotions that we may be running. If you no longer wish to receive these types of communications, you may turn them off at the device level.
Ads and Analytics
Currently, MorseVibes does not display any third-party ads. However, the app may include ads in the future. MorseVibes collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use particular features, to improve the app.
Information Usage
We use the information we collect to operate and improve our apps, and provide customer support. We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish MorseVibes' functionality. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, such as how many people use a particular feature with MorseVibes.
Messaging
MorseVibes utilizes Socket.io to provide real-time messaging functionality. As a part of this service, messages are temporarily stored and transmitted through our servers to facilitate conversation between users. Please note that while we make every effort to ensure the secure transmission of your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
In-app Purchases
MorseVibes offers consumable in-app purchases, specifically to change the color associated with your account. Purchases are non-refundable. If there is an issue with a purchase, users can contact support for resolution. However, please be aware that the service can be discontinued at any time, and we are not responsible for refunds if this occurs.
Security
We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure. All communication with the app requires HTTPS with certificate pinning. Passwords are encrypted and stored securely with Google Firebase. We are not responsible for any repercussions if our data is stolen.
Your Consent
By using MorseVibes, you consent to our privacy policy.
